About Me

My name is Zeke Lieberman and I am a music artist from the Philadelphia area. I release under the name Summer Thunder. I have a classical musical background and grew up playing the cello as well as drum kit, both of which I have been playing for over ten years. I also play guitar. I write my own music, where I specialize in rock and punk styles. I am also maintaining cello performance as a side hobby. I can play gigs such as weddings or recording cello tracks for songs. I am in the process of pursuing a minor in cello performance at Loyola University New Orleans. I am also studying music Industry at Loyola.
